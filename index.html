Dire Dawa Ras Hotel

About Us

Ras Hotel is the most well known Hotel in the Ethiopian hotel sector and has been for decades the landmark of the town. It has also longer history in customer service than most hotels in the city. The hotel has decent size rooms with air condition and spacious shower, clean bathroom as well as nice restaurant with amazing local and international dishes. Employees are friendly and one can't help but notice the management is right behind most operations too. It is one of the best hotels located in Dire Dawa City.
